OUR COMPANY HISTORY

iCuci Cleaning (formally known as JDT Cleaning) provides cleaning services in Johor Bahru. iCuci was founded in Sept 2016 and started its operation in Gelang Patah. It is owned by Cobridge Venture Sdn. Bhd and we are based in Iskandar Puteri, Johor. We served more than 1500 units of household/properties in Johor Bahru and the number is growing. We are also member of the Malaysia Association of Cleaning Contractors (MACC).

  • Vision: To clean 30000 properties and to ensure a comfortable, healthy living environment for everyone.
  • Mission: Ensuring cleanliness for our environment as a guide for healthy living for all.

Services

We clean services offerings include cleaning your living room, kitchen, bedrooms, bathrooms or office spaces. Our scope of work includes mopping, sweeping, ceiling fan wiping, cleaning windows, wiping grills, wiping furniture/sofa/appliances, cleaning toilets, car polishing, balcony cleaning, rubbish removal and common housekeeping services etc.
